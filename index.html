Research Interests

My research interests include machine intelligence and computer vision, with a focus on Uncertainty, Interpretability and Human-like Reasoning of AI models through learning structural representations. Moreover, my recent works mainly focus on medical image processing, especially about the retinal vessel segmentation on color fundus images.
