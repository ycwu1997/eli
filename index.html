Brief Intro.

I am a master candidate of Northwestern Polytechnical University (NPU). My current researches mainly focus on medical image processing, especially fine object segmentation such as retinal vessels via deep learning. My research interests include general computer vision, medical data processing, artificial intelligence and machine learning.
I have published three conference papers about retinal vessel segmentation. One paper has been early accepted by MICCAI 2019, which is the top conference of medical image analysis. Meanwhile I am one of the reviewers of MICCAI 2019, ISNN 2019 and Journal of Healthcare Engineering. Moreover, I am responsible for one Post-graduate Foundation of NPU and participate in two China Postdoctoral Science Foundations. Then I have applied one patent and two software copyrights.
